Welcome to The Galactic Garden!

The Goal: Grow a thriving cosmic ecosystem by forming stars, black holes, and constellations.

How to Play:
Move your mouse to attract tiny particles. As particles collide and merge, they gain mass.
When they reach a certain mass, they will form **stars**. Continue growing a star and it might form a **supernova** and collapse into a powerful **black hole**.
Don't worry, black holes eventually explode into new stars and particles.
And if enough stars come together, you can even name a **constellation**!
